How to use the TaxDome Help Center

TaxDome’s Help Center is your go-to resource for getting started, solving issues, and learning how to use the platform effectively.

Before you start

The TaxDome Help Center is open to everyone — no login needed. It lets you explore the platform at your own pace. Whether you’re just getting started or looking to fine-tune your workflow, you’ll find step-by-step guides, visuals, and practical tips for every part of TaxDome.

Your clients also have their own Client Help Center — a simple guide to help them use the client portal with ease.

Accessing the Help Center

You can get to the Help Center in two ways:

  1. Go to help.taxdome.com in your browser.
  2. Or, click the Help icon (the question mark) at the top-right corner of your portal. Type in what you’re looking for — like "pipelines," "upload documents," or "chats" — and you’ll be taken straight to the Help Center with matching results.

Note! If you can’t find what you’re looking for, try searching using a different term or explore by category.

Finding your way around an article

  • In the center of the page, you’ll see the article itself — this is where you’ll read the main content.
  • On the left, there’s a list of categories and a search bar. Use these to browse or look up other articles quickly.
  • On the right, you’ll see a list of sections from the article. Click any of them to jump straight to that part.
  • At the bottom of the page, you might find links to related articles that can help you learn more.

Getting started

We suggest starting with these beginner-friendly articles:

  • TaxDome beginner's guide — a complete introduction to setup, navigation, core tools, and learning options.
  • TaxDome learning resources — curated materials to help you build confidence as you explore the platform.
  • TaxDome Dictionary — a glossary of common terms and tools used across the platform.

If you need more help, you can always return to the Help Center to find additional articles or search for what you need.
